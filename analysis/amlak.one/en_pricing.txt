Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started

Is there a free trial?

Yes, we offer a permanent free plan that lets you try the platform at no cost. You can upgrade at any time.

How does payment work?

We support local and international payment methods. You can pay monthly or annually (with a discount for annual subscriptions).

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no extra fees. Your data will remain saved for 30 days after cancellation.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use SSL encryption and store data on secure servers. No third party has access to your data.

Does the system support Arabic?

Fully. Amlak was designed specifically for the Saudi market — Arabic interface, RTL layout, Saudi currency, and all local details.

What is the difference between plans?

Plans differ in the number of units, users, and available features. You can review the table above or contact us to help you choose the right plan.

Is there technical support?

Yes, our support team is available to assist you via the contact page. We commit to responding within 24 business hours.

Can I migrate my data from another system?

Yes, we provide assistance with importing basic data. Contact our team to discuss your migration requirements.
